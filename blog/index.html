Concepts

Why agents forget — and how learning fixes it
Concept
Every conversation starts from scratch. OpenClawBrain builds a persistent map of what your agent knows and updates it in the background from corrections and outcomes — so the next conversation starts smarter.
Context selection vs. keyword search
Concept
RAG finds text that looks similar. OpenClawBrain blends two signals — what the knowledge map knows about how facts connect, and what fits this specific question — so the right context surfaces even when the words don't match.
Learning from corrections
Concept
When your agent makes a mistake and you correct it, OpenClawBrain records what went wrong and updates its routing weights off the live path. The agent keeps running; the guide gets better.
OpenClaw vs. OpenClawBrain
Concept
OpenClaw is the runtime and orchestration layer. OpenClawBrain is the memory and context-selection layer that runs behind it. The agent keeps working if OpenClawBrain is unavailable — it's fail-open by design.
