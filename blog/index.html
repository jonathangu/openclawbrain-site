OpenClawBrain Blog

by Jonathan Gu

Your OpenClaw agent is smart, but it starts every conversation with amnesia. OpenClawBrain fixes that — it's a second brain that builds a map of what your agent knows, learns which knowledge matters for each question, and gets sharper from corrections. Think of a librarian who remembers every question you've ever asked. These posts explain how it works, what the benchmarks prove, and how to wire it up.
