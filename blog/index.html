OpenClawBrain Blog

by Jonathan Gu

Most AI agents retrieve knowledge like a keyword search — find the closest-sounding text, hope it's the right stuff. OpenClawBrain is more like a trail guide: it builds a map of everything your agent knows, learns which paths lead to good answers, and gets better from corrections. These posts explain how it works, what the benchmarks show, and how to set it up.
