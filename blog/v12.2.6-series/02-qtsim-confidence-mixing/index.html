Post 2: QTsim + Confidence Mixing

March 2026 · v12.2.6+ series

The router combines two policies: graph_prior and QTsim. The mix is dynamic, not fixed.

Signals

graph_prior captures durable structure. QTsim captures query-conditioned compatibility for the current turn.

Confidence Gate

The mixer uses uncertainty features from QTsim, especially entropy and margin.

lambda = sigmoid(a0 + a1 * margin - a2 * entropy)
pi = lambda * QTsim + (1 - lambda) * graph_prior

High margin + low entropy increases trust in QTsim. Low margin + high entropy shifts weight back to graph_prior.

Why It Matters

This avoids brittle behavior from fixed routing weights and makes fallback behavior explicit under uncertainty.
