Current technical-alpha truth

Distribution now

Use pnpm release:status and pnpm release:check against the public repo. The honest outside-consumer lane is repo tip plus freshly packed local tarballs.

Supported boundary

The promoted pack is the only supported compile boundary. Candidate packs stay off the hot path until activation stages and promotes them.

Package surface

Current packages are @openclawbrain/contracts, events, event-export, workspace-metadata, provenance, pack-format, activation, compiler, learner, and the optional @openclawbrain/openclaw bridge.

What OpenClawBrain proves in the public repo today

Promoted-pack runtime boundary

OpenClawBrain proves deterministic compile from the active promoted pack, explicit learned-route diagnostics such as routerIdentity and usedLearnedRouteFn, and promotion/rollback state instead of implied live mutation.

Checked-in proof lanes
